## Harvest
### Bulb Onions
- Tops fall over and dry
- Bulbs firm
- Stop watering 5-7 days before harvest
- Pull gently, do not wash

### Green Onions
- Harvest anytime usable
- Cut above soil line for regrowth

## Curing
- Lay in single layer in warm, shaded, ventilated area
- Cure 2-3 weeks
- Outer skins papery
- Trim roots, tops to 1 inch

## Storage
- Cool (32-50F), dry, dark, ventilated
- Mesh bags or braids
- Shelf life 2-4 months
- Keep away from potatoes

## Freezing
- Peel, chop, freeze raw
- Texture softens, use for cooking only
