## Harvest
- Head tight and dense
- Before yellow flowers
- Cut 5-6 inches below head
- Leave plant; side shoots develop
- Harvest early morning

## Short-Term Storage
- Do not wash
- Refrigerate in crisper drawer
- Use within 3-5 days
- Store loosely wrapped with dry paper towel

## Freezing
- Wash, cut florets
- Blanch 2-3 minutes, ice bath same duration
- Drain and dry
- Freeze single layer, then bag
- Storage: 8-12 months
- Cook from frozen

## Southern AZ Notes
- Cool-season crop only
- Bolts quickly in heat
- Provide shade cloth if necessary
