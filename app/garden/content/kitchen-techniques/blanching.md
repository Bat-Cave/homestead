## Definition
Blanching = brief boil -> immediate ice bath

### Purpose
- Stops enzyme activity
- Preserves flavor and color
- Maintains texture
- Prepares vegetables for freezing

## How to Blanch
1. Boil water
2. Add vegetables
3. Boil briefly (see timing)
4. Transfer immediately to ice bath
5. Cool completely
6. Drain and dry

### Timing Guide
- Snap peas: 60-90 sec
- Broccoli: 2-3 min
- Carrots (sliced): 2-3 min

Ice bath ~= boil time.

## After Blanching
- Dry thoroughly
- Freeze in single layer
- Transfer to airtight bag
- Remove air
- Cook from frozen
- Storage: 8-12 months
