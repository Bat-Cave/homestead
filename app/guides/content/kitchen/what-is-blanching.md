## Definition


Blanching = brief boil → immediate ice bath.


Purpose:
- Stops enzyme activity
- Preserves flavor
- Maintains texture
- Sets color
- Prepares vegetables for freezing


---


## How to Blanch


1. Bring large pot of water to rolling boil
2. Add vegetables
3. Boil briefly (see timing below)
4. Transfer immediately to ice bath
5. Cool completely
6. Drain and dry thoroughly


Ice bath time ≈ boil time.


---


## Common Timing Guide


- Snap peas: 60–90 seconds  
- Broccoli: 2–3 minutes  
- Carrots (sliced): 2–3 minutes  


---


## After Blanching


1. Dry completely
2. Spread in single layer
3. Freeze until solid
4. Transfer to airtight freezer bag
5. Remove excess air


Cook from frozen. Do not thaw.


Storage life: 8–12 months for best quality.
