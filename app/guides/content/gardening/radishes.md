## Harvest Timing
- 1-1.5 inches wide
- Harvest before woodiness from heat
- Pull gently
- Remove greens immediately (leave ~1/2 inch stem)

## Short-Term Storage
- Do not wash
- Brush off soil
- Store in airtight container with dry paper towel
- Refrigerate in crisper drawer
- Lasts 1-2 weeks

### Water Method
- Trim greens
- Submerge roots in cold water
- Refrigerate, change water every 1-2 days

## Pickling
- Quick pickle brine: 1 cup vinegar, 1 cup water, 1 tbsp sugar, 2 tsp salt
- Add optional spices: garlic, peppercorns, mustard seed
- Refrigerate 24 hours, keeps several weeks

## Fermentation
- 2-3% salt brine
- Submerge fully
- 5-10 days at room temp
- Refrigerate after

## Radish Greens
- Use within 2-3 days
- Store separately with paper towel
- Good for pesto, chimichurri, saute, soups
