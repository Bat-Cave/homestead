## When to Harvest


- Pods are bright green  
- 2–3 inches long  
- Plump but not bulging  
- Harvest in the morning  
- Pick every 1–2 days to increase production  


Use two hands: hold the vine, pinch the stem.


---


## Short-Term Storage (5–7 Days)


1. Do **not wash**
2. Store in breathable bag or container
3. Add dry paper towel
4. Keep in crisper drawer (high humidity)
5. Wash right before cooking


---


## Accumulating for a Stir Fry (Up to 2 Weeks)


- Harvest daily
- Store unwashed in fridge
- Inspect every few days
- Remove soft pods
- Replace damp paper towel


---


## Freezing Snap Peas (Best Long-Term Method)


### Step 1: Prep
- Wash
- Trim stem ends
- Remove strings if needed


### Step 2: Blanch
- Boil water
- Add peas for 60–90 seconds
- Transfer immediately to ice bath for equal time
- Drain completely


### Step 3: Freeze
- Dry thoroughly
- Lay flat on baking sheet
- Freeze until solid
- Transfer to airtight freezer bag
- Remove excess air


Cook directly from frozen.


Storage life: 8–12 months for best quality.
