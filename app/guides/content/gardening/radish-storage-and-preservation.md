## Harvest Timing


- 1–1.5 inches wide
- Harvest before heat causes woodiness
- Pull gently
- Remove greens immediately (leave ~½ inch stem)


Greens pull moisture from roots.


---


## Short-Term Storage (Up to 2 Weeks)


- Do not wash before storing
- Brush off soil
- Store in airtight container
- Add dry paper towel
- Keep in crisper drawer


If washed:
- Dry completely
- Expect 7–10 days shelf life


---


## Water Storage Method (High Crisp Retention)


1. Trim greens
2. Submerge radishes in jar of cold water
3. Refrigerate
4. Change water every 1–2 days


---


## Pickled Radishes (Recommended)


### Quick Pickle Brine


- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 cup water
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tsp salt
- Optional: garlic, peppercorns, mustard seed


Heat brine.  
Pour over sliced radishes.  
Refrigerate 24 hours before eating.  


Keeps several weeks refrigerated.


---


## Fermented Radishes


- 2–3% salt brine by weight
- Fully submerge
- Ferment 5–10 days at room temperature
- Refrigerate after fermentation


---


## Radish Greens


Use within 2–3 days.  
Store separately with paper towel.  


Good for:
- Pesto
- Chimichurri
- Sauté
- Soups
