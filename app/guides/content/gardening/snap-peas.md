## Harvesting
- Pods bright green, 2-3 inches long, plump but not bulging
- Harvest in the morning
- Pick every 1-2 days
- Use two hands: hold vine, pinch stem

## Short-Term Storage (5-7 Days)
- Do not wash
- Store in breathable bag or container
- Add dry paper towel
- Keep in crisper drawer
- Wash right before cooking

## Accumulating for Stir Fry (Up to 2 Weeks)
- Harvest daily
- Store unwashed in fridge
- Inspect every few days
- Remove soft pods
- Replace damp paper towel

## Freezing (Long-Term)
### Step 1: Prep
- Wash
- Trim stems
- Remove strings if needed

### Step 2: Blanch
- Boil 60-90 seconds
- Ice bath same duration
- Drain thoroughly

### Step 3: Freeze
- Dry completely
- Lay flat on tray
- Freeze solid
- Transfer to airtight bag, remove air

Cook directly from frozen. Storage: 8-12 months.
